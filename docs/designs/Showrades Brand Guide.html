01 — Brand

Brand Personality

Showrades lives at the intersection of silly and smart — every interaction should make someone laugh or say "ooh, I knew that one!" It bridges ages without talking down to either end.

🎭
Playful
Every tap should feel like part of the game, not the app.
⚡
Energetic
Snappy feedback. Motion that buzzes with excitement.
🌍
Inclusive
Designed for a 4-year-old and their grandparent at the same table.
✦
Minimal
One clear action per screen. Colour adds joy, not noise.
