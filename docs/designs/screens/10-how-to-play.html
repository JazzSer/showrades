How to Play
Charades, the easy way
Act it out, no talking. Your team shouts guesses before time runs out. Here's the whole game in four moves.
1
🎭Act it out
One player holds the phone and acts out the card — no talking, no sounds, and no pointing at real objects in the room.
2
💬Team guesses
Teammates shout out as many guesses as they can before the timer hits zero. Everyone else stays quiet.
3
✓Got It or Pass
Guessed right? Tap Got It for a point and jump to the next card. Stuck? Tap Pass to skip — no penalty.
4
🤝Pass the phone
When the timer ends, hand the phone to the next team and keep the laughs going. Scores are tallied for you.

Two ways to play

🖼️
Picture
Under 7
Big illustrated prompts. No reading needed, so little ones can lead the room.
🔤
Word
8 & up
Large, easy-to-read word prompts for older kids, teens and grown-ups.
Both modes share the same teams, scores and timer — a 5-year-old and a grandparent can play in the very same game.
First team to the points target wins — or pick Endless in setup and the highest score takes the crown.
